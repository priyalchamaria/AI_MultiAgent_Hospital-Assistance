Policy Title: Discharge Policy
Policy ID: HOSP-POL-003
Notice: Synthetic academic policy. This document is generated for internship project demonstration only.

Purpose:
Ensure patients leave with safe instructions, medication guidance, and follow-up planning.

Scope:
Applies to all discharged inpatients and observation patients.

Responsibilities:
Clinicians complete discharge summaries. Nurses review instructions. Case management supports high-risk discharge needs.

Procedure:
Discharge planning begins at admission. Before discharge, staff verify diagnosis, medication changes, pending tests, follow-up appointments, transport, and home-care needs.

Exceptions:
Patients leaving against medical advice must receive risk counseling and available written instructions.

Escalation Process:
If a patient lacks a safe home environment, medication access, or required equipment, staff must escalate to case management before discharge.
