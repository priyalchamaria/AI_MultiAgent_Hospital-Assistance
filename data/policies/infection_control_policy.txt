Policy Title: Infection Control Policy
Policy ID: HOSP-POL-007
Notice: Synthetic academic policy. This document is generated for internship project demonstration only.

Purpose:
Minimize healthcare-associated infections among patients, visitors, and staff.

Scope:
Applies to patient-care areas, shared equipment, isolation rooms, procedure areas, and staff workspaces.

Responsibilities:
All staff perform hand hygiene. Department leaders monitor compliance. Infection prevention staff audit and coach teams.

Procedure:
Hand hygiene is required before and after patient contact, after exposure to bodily fluids, after removing gloves, and before clean procedures. Shared equipment must be disinfected between patients.

Exceptions:
Emergency care may begin before full isolation setup if delay would endanger the patient.

Escalation Process:
Suspected outbreaks or repeated non-compliance must be escalated to infection prevention leadership.
