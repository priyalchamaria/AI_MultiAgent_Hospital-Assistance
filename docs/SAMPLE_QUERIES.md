# Sample Queries

## Structured Patient Data

- How many patients are in the database?
- How many emergency admissions were there?
- How many abnormal test results were recorded?
- What is the average billing amount?
- What is the average billing amount by insurance provider?
- Show the top 5 hospitals with the most cancer patients.
- How many diabetes patients were admitted in 2022?
- What is the average length of stay by admission type?
- How many patients are there by gender?
- Which medications are most common?

## Hospital Policy Documents

- What is the patient privacy policy?
- What should staff do if they suspect a privacy incident?
- What is the discharge planning policy?
- When should case management be contacted before discharge?
- Summarize the medication reconciliation policy.
- What is required for high-alert medications?
- When should abnormal test results be escalated?
- What are the hand hygiene requirements?
- How should billing corrections be documented?
- Can staff promise insurance coverage decisions?

## Demo Script

1. Ask a structured question and point out that the Orchestrator routes to SQL.
2. Open the SQL expander and explain the generated query.
3. Ask a policy question and point out that the Orchestrator routes to RAG.
4. Open the evidence expander and show the retrieved source document.
5. Explain how the system combines structured and unstructured hospital knowledge in one interface.
